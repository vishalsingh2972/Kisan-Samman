# 🌾 Kisan Samman – Voice-First AI Agent for Farmers

Kisan Samman is a multilingual voice-first AI assistant that helps Indian farmers discover, understand, and access government welfare schemes in under 30 seconds.

---

## 🚜 Problem

The Indian government spends over **₹1.8 lakh crore annually** on farmer welfare programs, yet millions of eligible farmers miss out on benefits due to:

* Low awareness of available schemes
* Complex government portals
* Language barriers
* Confusing eligibility criteria
* Lack of application guidance

---

## 💡 Solution

Kisan Samman makes government schemes accessible through a simple voice conversation.

A farmer can ask:

> "क्या मेरे राज्य में ड्रिप सिंचाई के लिए कोई सब्सिडी है?"

The AI agent will:

* ✅ Find relevant government schemes
* ✅ Check eligibility based on farmer details
* ✅ Provide official application links
* ✅ Locate the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC)
* ✅ Explain benefits and application steps in the farmer's preferred language
* ✅ Complete the entire guidance flow in under 30 seconds
